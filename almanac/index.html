Daylight swing vs latitude

Annual amplitude of daylight length (longest minus shortest day) against absolute latitude, all 38 locations. Longitude has no say in the swing; it only slides the clock underneath (the Kolkata-Mumbai spread). Click a point to switch location.

Any latitude

The same shaded year chart at an arbitrary latitude, on local mean solar time (no zone, no DST, sun highest at 12:00 minus the equation of time). Slide to explore; polar day and night appear beyond the circles.

? Two slider modes: absolute latitude in degrees (0–90, northern hemisphere), or the pole scale, latitude / 90, running −1 (South Pole) through 0 (equator) to +1 (North Pole), linear. The readout shows pole value first, exact degrees in parentheses. The chart uses local mean solar time, so it is pure astronomy: no time zone, no DST, no longitude.
